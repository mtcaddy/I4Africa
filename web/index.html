Surpass the Curve

FACT: COVID-19 virus is already present in Africa and it is spreading.

What we CAN do is prepare and learn from those who have experienced the wave and adopt to cultural and environmental realities across the continent.

Why

At a global level

Because COVID19 has infected large populations in Europe and USA, medical supplies that are necessary for doctors who will be in contact with COVID19 patients and help citizen protect themselves are short in availability.
They are NOT possible to produce and import from countries that normally supplied them.Therefore, solutions will be necessary to prevent the virus from spreading and to protect our communities and families.

What

  • Mobilize national and local actors that can provide solutions
  • Adopt creative measures to utilize the resources available for that which will become scarce : Personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Provide clear consistent product blueprint to makers to ensure quality and consistency
  • Create connected communication channels between production and demand

About Us

Ubuntu - Together we can do it!

Unify knowledge, skills and resources that can help low income and vulnerable local communities in Africa to be self-sufficient with quick responsive and responsible action to protect and surpass the curve of the COVID19 spread. By learning how to use the already available material to make adequate personal protection, not only can local citizens mobilize this knowledge to protect themselves and their communities, but also ensure the essential basic needs to protect their families against a virus that knows no boundaries.
Decentrally organized - Locally applied
